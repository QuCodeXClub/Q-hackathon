# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Offer actionable feedback.
- Be collaborative and professional.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks, insults, or trolling.
- Public or private abuse of any participant.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to repository interactions, issues, pull requests, discussions, and related community channels.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private report with maintainers through repository security/contact channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
